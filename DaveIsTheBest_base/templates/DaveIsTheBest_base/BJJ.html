I started doing BJJ in 2019 and got hooked pretty quick. I just have so much fun practicing. In fact, it is common for me to burst into laughter during sparring, usually when my partner hits some effortless sweep or something.

BJJ has had quite an impact on my life. I train almost exclusively in the mornings which is a great way to start a day. I'm sure the exercise alone helps but fighting out of a rear naked choke sure helps to put things into perspective prior to facing a day at the office.
